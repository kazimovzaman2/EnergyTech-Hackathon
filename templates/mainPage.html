Personalized Consumption Insights

The program will leverage machine learning algorithms to analyze monthly and daily energy, gas, and water usage data and provide users with personalized insights into their consumption patterns.

Goal Setting and Tracking

EnergyTech will allow users to set monthly and weekly consumption goals and track their progress toward meeting those goals over time.

Automated Meter Reading

To ensure accurate and efficient collection of usage data, the application proposes that utility companies switch to an automated meter reading system.

Consumption Notifications

EcoTrack will automatically send notifications via SMS or email to remind users when they are approaching their monthly consumption limits.

Personalized Recommendations.

The application will include machine learning capabilities that will enable it to provide personalized recommendations on how to reduce energy, gas, and water usage based on individual user habits and consumption patterns.
